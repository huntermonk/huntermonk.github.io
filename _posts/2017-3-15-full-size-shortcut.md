To full-size a window in macOS, just press:

```
 ⌘ + ⌃ + F
```

That will change your window from

![Remote culture](/img/full-size/small.png)

to

![Remote culture](/img/full-size/full.png)

_(⌘ is command & ⌃ is control)_
